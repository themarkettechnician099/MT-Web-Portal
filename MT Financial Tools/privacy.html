Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

The Market Technician, as a collective entity, we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Service, we collect the following types of information:

  • Authentication Data: Your email address and a securely hashed version of your password when you create an account.
  • Financial Sandbox Data: Numerical inputs, risk parameters, and portfolio simulations you enter into our tools (e.g., Wealth Sandbox, Strike Planner).
  • Payment Verification Data: Information you submit via our Google Forms payment portal, which may include your name, contact details, and screenshots of payment receipts (GCash, Bank Transfer) required to unlock Premium access.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to provide and improve the Service:

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To save your financial parameters so your sandbox environments load seamlessly when you return.
  • To verify your subscription tier (Trial vs. Premium) and manage your access expiration dates.
  • To communicate essential account updates, such as password resets.

3. Data Storage and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We rely on enterprise-grade third-party infrastructure to secure your information:

  • Google Firebase: Your authentication credentials and sandbox data are stored in Google Firebase Firestore. Firebase utilizes industry-standard encryption protocols to protect your data at rest and in transit.
  • Google Workspace: Payment verification submissions are securely processed and stored within our private Google Sheets environment.

4. Your Data Privacy Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012, you possess strict rights regarding your personal data. The Market Technician empowers you to exercise these rights directly within the app:

  • The Right to Erasure: You have the right to demand the total erasure of your data. We have built a "Nuclear Delete" feature into your user dashboard. Using this function will permanently erase your Authentication profile and all associated sandbox data from our Firebase servers. This action cannot be undone.
  • The Right to Access & Rectification: You may update your financial parameters at any time by simply overriding the inputs in the sandboxes.

5. Security of Your Data

While we use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password. We will never ask for your password via email or customer support.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data privacy rights, please contact us at: themarkettechnician099@gmail.com.
