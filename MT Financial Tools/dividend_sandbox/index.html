The Guidebook

Master your portfolio, understand the mechanics, and grow your wealth.

Part 1: The Purpose

The MT Dividend Sandbox is your risk-free financial terminal. It is designed to act as a dual-purpose engine: it tracks the exact cash flow and allocations of your real-world dividend assets, while simultaneously simulating decades of compound growth so you can map out your financial independence.

Part 2: The Pillars & Operations

1. The Dashboard (Global View)

Purpose: A bird's-eye view of your entire wealth.
Operation: This tab is strictly read-only. It automatically parses all data from your Ledgers and Allocator to build visual charts, track unrealized PnL, and summarize your holdings.

2. The Allocator (The Trading Desk)

Purpose: Where you execute buy/sell orders and input market data.
Operation: Input the ticker, dividend per share, and market price. Toggle the "BUY" or "SELL" buttons to reveal the order ticket. If a company announces a new dividend, simply update the Div Per Share field, leave the ticket on "HOLD", and click the commit button to instantly update your portfolio yield without selling.

3. The Ledgers (The Bank)

Purpose: The immutable history of your cash and trades.
Operation: Click "+ Manage Funds" to inject or withdraw virtual cash. To reverse a mistake, simply click the "✕" on any ledger row to mathematically reverse the cash and share impact.

4. Projections (The Time Machine)

Purpose: A sandbox within the sandbox to simulate decades of compound growth based on your current real-world portfolio.
Operation: Slide the "Stop Age" to map out Coast FIRE (when you stop adding new money). The engine dynamically tells you exactly what monthly top-up you need to achieve your Retirement Income Goal.

5. Div Calendar (Cash Flow Mapping)

Purpose: Visualizing exactly when you get paid.
Operation: Go to the Allocator tab, find your stock, and click the months under "Payout Schedule". The Calendar will instantly build a month-by-month cash flow map. You can also toggle to view how massive these monthly payouts will become by your retirement age.

Part 3: The Wealth Playbook

🔄 The Power of Reinvestment

Getting paid dividends is great. Using those dividends to buy more shares is where wealth is actually built. When your dividends buy more shares, those new shares pay you even more dividends next year. The Sandbox assumes you are strictly reinvesting 100% of your payouts. That’s what turns a flat savings account into a parabolic, exponential curve.

📈 The Yield Reality (Don't fall for the trap)

In the Philippine market, good businesses are remarkably consistent. A 5% to 7% dividend yield is perfectly reasonable, and even 8% can be very safe if the business is strong. However, chasing a huge yield (like 12% or 15%) is often dangerous. Usually, an abnormally high yield means the stock price has crashed because the underlying business is failing. A solid 5-6% yield paired with consistent, boring top-ups is how you win the game safely.

💧 Consistency over Timing

The Planned Top-Up slider in the Projections tab is your most powerful tool. You don't need to perfectly time the bottom of the market. Adding a little bit of fresh capital every single month—whether the market is red or green—smooths out your average cost and massively accelerates your timeline. Boring, consistent investing creates exciting, life-changing wealth.
